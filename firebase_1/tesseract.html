What is a tesseract?

Tesseracts are, in essence, 4 dimensional cubes (Title Drop). They are also made up of cubes themselves. Think of the difference between a square and a cube. A cube has six faces and each face is a square. Tesseracts do the same, except they are made up of 8 cubes.
